Position Title: History Teacher
Location: New York City, NY
Employment Type: Full-time
Salary: $60,000 - $75,000 per year

Overview:
We are seeking a passionate and knowledgeable History Teacher to join our high school faculty. The ideal candidate will have a strong academic background in History or a related field, experience in secondary education, and a commitment to fostering critical thinking, civic awareness, and engagement with the past.

Key Responsibilities:
Teach U.S. History and World History to students in grades 9–12.
Develop lesson plans in accordance with curriculum standards.
Create an inclusive and engaging classroom environment.
Assess and monitor student progress.
Collaborate with colleagues, parents, and school administrators.
Participate in extracurricular activities related to social studies.

Requirements:
Bachelor's degree or higher in History, Education, or related field.
Teaching certification for grades 9–12 (or eligibility).
Minimum of 2 years teaching experience preferred.
Excellent communication, organization, and interpersonal skills.
Familiarity with project-based and inquiry-based learning is a plus.
